The Student Chapter of Central Louisiana is a youth-led program supported by the Home Builders Association of Central Louisiana. We connect high school students with real-world experiences in the homebuilding industry — from job site visits and internships to scholarships and career training.

This program gives students from Tioga and Grant High Schools the tools, mentorship, and opportunities they need to graduate confidently and pursue meaningful careers in construction, real estate, architecture, finance, and more.

“These kids aren’t just learning how to build homes. They’re building futures — and our support helps lay every brick.”

Whether you're a local business, community member, or industry leader, you can help change the course of a student's life. Your involvement — big or small — provides more than support; it offers belief, opportunity, and a future worth reaching for.
