    FAQ

    Quick answers about the Skeet & Scholars Classic.

    Who can compete?
    Individuals and teams of four are welcome. Novice through experienced shooters may participate.
    Are minors allowed?
    Yes, with a parent/guardian on site and signed waiver. All youth must follow range safety rules and staff directions.
    What equipment do I need?
    A shotgun (12/20 gauge recommended), eye/ear protection, and ammo. Limited loaner gear may be available—ask at check-in.
    How do payments work?
    After you submit the form, you’ll receive a secure QuickBooks payment link by email. Pay online to confirm your spot.
    What’s the schedule?
    Check-in 8:00 AM · Safety brief 8:30 · First shots 9:00 AM · Awards immediately after rounds conclude.
    Rain or refund policy?
    The event is rain-or-shine unless the range closes for safety. If postponed, all registrations roll to the new date.
    How do I sponsor or donate prizes?
    Email info@studentchapterofcenla.org and we’ll send the sponsor packet with levels and prize needs.
