About the Chapter

We are the youth extension of the Home Builders Association of Central Louisiana, committed to providing safe, affordable housing and promoting the homebuilding industry. Our mission includes advocacy, education, and leadership development for high school students through real-world experiences and community involvement.

Scholarships

Scholarship contributions start at $1,000 with a 2-year minimum commitment. Funds are paid directly to the student upon meeting all senior-year qualifications outlined in the application. Scholarships are awarded through local sponsorships and HBACL fundraising events, such as the annual Aim for Education Skeet Shoot.

To view all requirements, deadlines, eligibility guidelines, and expectations, please review the full 2025 Student Chapter Scholarship Guidelines.
