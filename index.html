About the Chapter

We are the youth extension of the Home Builders Association of Central Louisiana, committed to providing safe, affordable housing and promoting the homebuilding industry. Our mission includes advocacy, education, and leadership development for high school students through real-world experiences and community involvement.

Help Us Build a Brighter Future – Donate Today

Every gift you make — no matter the size — helps unlock life-changing opportunities for students right here in our local community.

Through your generosity, young leaders from Tioga and Grant High Schools gain access to scholarships, real-world career experiences, mentorships, and service projects that shape their futures and strengthen Central Louisiana.

Your donation isn't just a contribution — it's an investment in a student's dream, a family's future, and our community's tomorrow.

“Every nail they drive, every foundation they lay, and every dream they build is made possible by people like you.”

Our Schools

The Student Chapter currently operates in partnership with two high schools:

  • Tioga High School – Career and technical education programs building confidence and real-world skills. Students benefit from service, career, and leadership experiences in construction and agriculture.
  • Grant High School – Actively engaged in student chapter activities, professional development, and industry networking.

Scholarships

Scholarship contributions start at $1,000 with a 2-year minimum commitment. Funds are paid directly to the student upon meeting all senior-year qualifications outlined in the application. Scholarships are awarded through local sponsorships and HBACL fundraising events, such as the annual Aim for Education Skeet Shoot.
