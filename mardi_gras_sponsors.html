Float Sponsorship

Partner with the Student Chapter of Central Louisiana to put your brand in front of tens of thousands of parade-goers while supporting nearly 100 students from Tioga, Grant, and Jena. Complete the form below and we’ll follow up with your invoice and event details.

Sponsorship Levels

  • Float Partnership – $1,500 Major float signage, brand recognition along the route, and merch toss privileges.
  • Brand Placement – $500 Professionally printed sign on the float + merch toss privileges.
  • Participant Sponsor – $250 Support the chapter and join students during the parade with merch toss privileges (no float signage).

HBACL members receive a $100 discount on all sponsorships. All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law; receipts are provided upon processing.
