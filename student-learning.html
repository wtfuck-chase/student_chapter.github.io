About NAHB E-Learning

The National Association of Home Builders (NAHB) offers an extensive library of webinars, certification courses, and business education exclusively for members. These online resources are designed to help students and professionals in the building industry gain practical skills and credentials recognized nationwide.

Who Can Access

All active NAHB members have full access to NAHB’s e-learning platform. If you are a Student Chapter member, your membership includes NAHB benefits — so you already have access!

If you're not yet a member but would like access, you can apply to join the Home Builders Association of Central Louisiana (HBACL), and we will set up your NAHB membership and PIN.
